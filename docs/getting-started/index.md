!!! tip "The single most important mindset"

    You are the **leader**, not the laborer. Your early hand-crafting exists only to bootstrap a settlement. The moment you have citizens, your job becomes *organizing their work* — assigning jobs, marking work areas, directing research, and keeping everyone fed and happy. If you find yourself mining for hours, stop and ask which citizen should be doing it instead.
