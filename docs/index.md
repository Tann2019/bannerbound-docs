## What is Bannerbound?

Bannerbound is a **civilization-building modpack** for Minecraft. It rebuilds the game around one idea: *you lead, you don't solo.* Instead of mining and crafting everything by hand forever, you gather your first tools from flint and bone, found a settlement around a humble campfire, and then **your citizens do the work** — foraging, farming, felling trees, smelting bronze, and raising walls — while you direct research, labor, territory, faith, and war from the town hall.

It runs on two mods that work as one:

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-crown-outline:{ .lg .middle } &nbsp; **Bannerbound: Core**

    ---

    The civilization engine. Settlements and citizens with real needs, jobs and a labor board, a research tree across nine eras, faith drawn from constellations, government and elections, territory, diplomacy, trade, war, and crises.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore the civilization](civilization/index.md)

-   :material-hammer-wrench:{ .lg .middle } &nbsp; **Bannerbound: Antiquity**

    ---

    The Stone-to-Bronze-Age tech content. Knap flint tools, weave baskets, fire pottery in a kiln, cast and forge bronze at the bloomery, tan leather, brew grog, and tip your darts with poison.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Master the crafts](antiquity/index.md)

</div>
