# The Civilization

<p class="bb-lead">Everything in Bannerbound orbits the settlement. It is at once your home, your government, your economy, and your army — a living thing with a population to feed, a mood to manage, a border to defend, and a history it will remember long after you log off.</p>

The **Core** mod is the engine that makes all of this run. This section explains each system on its own page, but they are deeply interlinked: happy citizens work faster, work produces food and science, science unlocks eras, eras widen your borders, and borders bring you into contact with neighbors who may become trading partners — or enemies.
